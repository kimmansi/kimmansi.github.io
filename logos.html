Logos

I am a Logo nerd

Sitting with a client and discussing how they envision their company...the images, colors, and feelings that their mission and name mean to them...is the most exciting part of the whole branding process for me. I immediately start picturing what elements can be pulled out and enhanced, what colors truly match the words they respond to most, and how all those parts can be translated into imagery that says everything they want to say about their brand. Adjusting a single line or dot...or changing a color one hue lighter or darker can transform the whole message of a logo. It's an incredibly powerful graphic to work on as a designer. I just love it!

Divine Investments Real Estate Logo

Before & After

The client for this logo had some clear ideas for the end product regarding color scheme and capturing a theme of "hard and soft". The pink and black colors and the triangle were important parts of the existing brand and they definitely wanted those elements to be included in the new logo. They also shared that an important part of their client base were investors from all walks of life. Their mission was to open real estate investing and other investing products to people who may not otherwise think those products were available to them for investment. I immediately thought to create a graphic that highlighted the real estate element of their business product as that was the investment vehicle they felt contributed the most to the growth of wealth and also the one that people were least likely to think was open to them. I also decided to only present first logo iterations in the colors they specified. Normally I would still throw in an alternative vision. But after researching their local market and seeing how these colors would absolutely help them differentiate themselves from their competitors and defintely hearing during client interview how intrinsic these colors were to their brand already, it was a no-brainer to make these work.
